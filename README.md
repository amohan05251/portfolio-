# Personal Portfolio

Welcome to my personal portfolio! This website serves as a collection of my academic, technical, and extracurricular experiences as a Computer Science and Mathematics student at the University of British Columbia (UBC).

🌐 Live Website: https://amohan05251.github.io/personal-portfolio/

### About

I am pursuing a Combined Major in Computer Science and Mathematics at UBC. My interests span several areas of computing and mathematics, including software development, web development, data analysis, algorithms, and research. As these fields require a strong foundation, I actively explore opportunities both within and beyond my university curriculum to continuously strengthen my technical knowledge and problem-solving skills.

This portfolio reflects my journey as a student developer and highlights projects that demonstrate my ability to apply technical concepts to real-world problems. Alongside coursework, I enjoy participating in volunteer initiatives, case competitions, and personal projects that challenge me to learn new technologies and think creatively.

### About This Website

This portfolio was designed and developed entirely using HTML5 and CSS3. Rather than relying on frameworks or website builders, I chose to build the site from scratch to strengthen my understanding of core front-end development principles, including semantic HTML, responsive layouts, and modern CSS styling.
